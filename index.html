About Us

When it comes to love, real love, we at Porch & Pearls agree wholeheartedly with Carrie Bradshaw: we believe in "ridiculous, inconvenient, consuming, 'can’t live without each other' love." Know what shouldn't be "ridiculous, inconvenient, and consuming?" Planning your wedding day. That's where we come in.

With Porch & Pearls, you‘ll have a coordinator who exudes passion, perfectionism, and unparalleled creativity and resourcefulness. We know that a truly magnificent special event begins long before the guests arrive; it begins with masterful planning. Our relentless concentration on details and our seamless delivery from start to finish will keep you on schedule and focused on realizing your most perfect day imaginable. With offerings from full-scale, glamorous galas, to intimate, tender soirees, Porch & Pearls brings exceptional service, experienced etiquette, and customized coordination to each and every couple.

From the smallest detail to the grandest event, Porch & Pearls specializes in weddings and celebrations in Philadelphia and the surrounding suburbs, but our team is available to travel to the Jersey shore and points elsewhere in Pennsylvania.

Services

Day-Of Coordination

For couples who have done all the planning for their wedding, Porch & Pearls offers day-of coordination. But, who are we kidding? It‘s really month-of coordination! After our initial consultation at the time of booking, we‘ll make additional vendor recommendations (if you need them), complete a customized wedding detail plan and day-of itinerary, serve as the point of contact and confirm all vendors prior to your big day. This level of service includes up to 12 hours of wedding day coordination and unlimited emails and phone calls leading up to your event. With a Porch & Pearls coordinator watching over the details of the day, you - and those closest to you - can enjoy every meaningful moment without any of the stress.

Partial Planning

Porch & Pearls‘ partial planning package (pheww! say that three times fast!) includes all of the services of day-of coordination, plus monthly check-ins to ensure you‘re on track with your planning, review and revision of all wedding stationery, and on-site rehearsal attendance and supervision. You still get to do your own planning, but with a little more support and guidance along the way.

Full Planning

With our full-service wedding planning packing, Porch & Pearls will guide you through the entire process, create the overall wedding design, attend vendor meetings, project and manage your budget, maintain the schedule of when vendor payments are due, manage RSVPs, put together favors and out-of-town guests‘ welcome packages, help create the seating chart and floor plan, and of course, we‘ll oversee all rehearsal and day-of staging to make your dream day a reality.
